High Performance and The Cs

High-performance organizations (teams) aren’t lucky accidents. They are developed and led by competent, confident and consistent high-performance leaders. These leaders understand that a powerful strategy and structure are necessary for the organization to succeed, yet they are insufficient without an appropriate / healthy culture.

Leadership is taking people places they wouldn’t go on their own. Leadership is getting people to achieve and do what they wouldn’t achieve and do on their own. The key to effective leadership is the practice of self-awareness, empathy, trust, and continuous learning. Leaders are fueled by an inner drive that’s visible as authentic commitment that gets channeled through their utilization of candor, courage, communication, and collaboration.

Leaders are positive influencers. Leaders trade in trust. Leaders know that trust is the primary currency for effective relationships of all kinds. Trust is the product of connection, inspiration, personal and organizational alignment. With trust, leaders galvanize an organization for its possibilities. Trust overcomes fear, defensiveness, cynicism, and apathy -- the factors that destroy hope, meaningful action, and empowered execution. A good leader is anyone who works to make a positive difference for individuals and teams in support of achieving a clear mission.

Leaders model the way, encourage the heart and inspire people to leave their comfort zones for something potentially better, bigger and or bolder. Leaders create the conditions for people to find a way or make a way to deliver results that make the difference.

Gregg Baron, CMC

Gregg Baron, CMC, is an author, sales trainer and certified management consultant*. His expertise is in the areas of enhancing sales performance, the art of positive influence, and the leadership practices for leading change. Some of his clients include Honeywell, Verizon, CIGNA, Cisco Systems, Dedicated Micros, Anixter, Delta Airlines, Royal Caribbean Cruises and Celebrity Cruises.

His first book, 250 Ways to Increase Your Top and Bottom Lines, was published by Dartnell. Leadership without Excuses: How to Create Accountability and High Performance was published by McGraw-Hill. Mr. Baron has also been published in a variety of professional journals on topics such as service quality, leadership and influence practices. He is one of fifty contributing authors to the American Marketing Association’s Marketing Encyclopedia: Issues and Trends Shaping the Future published by NTC.

In addition to his research and writing, Gregg has hosted his own video show, Successful Business Relationships, on a major airline. He founded Success Sciences, headquartered in Tampa.

Gregg and his team created an extraordinary simulation learning platform called Communication Coach. The system uniquely delivers the Performance Conditioning® required to rapidly develop optimal competence, confidence and consistency. Just what you need for brand-driven service, brand-driven sales experiences and coaching conversations that significantly impact performance. They also developed a social learning app, comCOACHgo, primarily for sales and service teams.

*CMC® (Certified Management Consultant) is the certification mark awarded by the Institute of Management Consultants USA and represents meeting the highest global standards and ethical canons of the profession. Fewer than 1% of all consultants have achieved this level of excellence.
